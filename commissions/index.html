Commissions

My commissions open regularly! Once I've cleared my current queue at any given time, I will post a commission form on my Twitter account (@RelaxableArt) and leave it open for 24 hours.

Why can’t you accept every submission?

I reserve the right to decline a commission for any reason; if an idea falls under anything in the "Will not draw" part of my terms, I will have a discussion with the commissioner first to iron out some details. If a compromise cannot be reached, the commission will be declined.


Before filling out the form, please be sure to read my Terms of Service, where you will find a list of things I am willing and not willing to draw and other important information. If you have any questions regarding anything in my terms, please do not hesitate to send me an email at inquiries@relaxable.art or contact me directly on Discord: Relaxable#1073.


Detailed information on my pricing can be found on the pricing page.
