Commissions

My commissions will open every week at 17:00 UTC on Sundays! I will post a commission form on my Twitter account (@RelaxableArt) and leave it open until Monday morning, where I will review applications and contact those I have chosen to work with. This form will not be operating on a first come, first serve basis.

Why can’t you accept every application?

There is a lot of demand, and not every idea works well with my style and artistic direction — this is in the interest of being fair to everyone who applies. I will select the prompts that are a good fit for my set of skills, and the art will look best for it!


Before filling out the form, please be sure to read my Terms of Service, where you will find a list of things I am willing and not willing to draw and other important information. If you have any questions regarding anything in my terms, please do not hesitate to send me an email at inquiries@relaxable.art or contact me directly on Discord: Relaxable#2400.


Detailed information on my prices can be found on the pricing page.
